Crypto, or cryptocurrency, is a digital currency that uses
cryptography to work as a medium of exchange without a
central authority like a bank or government.
Cryptocurrencies are not physical, and are instead recorded
in a digital ledger that uses cryptography to secure transactions and
control the creation of new coins.
